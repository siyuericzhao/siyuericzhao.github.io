"New Sheriffs In Town"

The 2019 NBA offseason started with the blockbuster Anthony Davis trade to the Lakers. With Brooklyn Nets acquiring Kevin Durant and the LA Clippers signing the Finals MVP Kawhi Leonard, these three teams suddenly became championship contenders.

"Battling Through The Regular Season"

As teams battling for the playoffs positions during this regular season, the LA rival Lakers and Clippers are on there way of getting the top two seeds in the West Conference. However, the much anticipated Brooklyn Nets team did not meet the expectation mainly due to injury of their two super star player Kevin Durant and Kyrie Irving.

"COVID, The Orlando Bubble, Longest Season"

2020 has been a challenge year for the whole world. Due to the sever impact of the pandemic, the NBA has been shut down for a long period of time and thus suffered tremendous amount of loss.

"Mamba Out"

On January 26, 2020, Kobe Bryant, the NBA legend who led the Los Angeles Lakers to five championships over a 20-year career, was killed in a helicopter crash along with other 8 people including his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna. Although Kobe is no longer with us, his 'Mamba Mentality' remains and will keep inspire and motivate all of us to forge ahead in life despite the challenges ans hardships we might face.
