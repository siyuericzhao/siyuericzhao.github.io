"New Sheriffs In Town"

The 2019 NBA offseason started with the blockbuster Anthony Davis trade to the LA Lakers. With Brooklyn Nets acquiring Kevin Durant and the LA Clippers signing the 2019 Finals MVP Kawhi Leonard, these three teams suddenly became championship contenders.

"Battling Through The Regular Season"

As teams battling for the playoffs positions during this regular season, the LA rival Lakers and Clippers are on there way of getting the top two seeds in the Western Conference. However, the much anticipated Brooklyn Nets team did not meet the expectation mainly due to injury of their two super star player Kevin Durant and Kyrie Irving, they only ranked 7th in the Eastern Conference with merely 35 wins.

"COVID, The Orlando Bubble, Longest Season"

2020 has been a challenge year for the whole world. Due to the severe impact of the pandemic, the NBA has been forced to shut down for a long period of time and thus suffered from tremendous loss.

#24 "Mamba Out" #8

On January 26, 2020, Kobe Bryant, the NBA legend who led the Los Angeles Lakers to five championships over a 20-year career, was killed in a helicopter crash along with other 8 people including his 13-year-old daughter, Gianna. Although Kobe is no longer with us, his 'Mamba Mentality' remains and will keep inspire and motivate all of us to forge ahead in life despite the challenges and hardships we might face.

Kobe's Oscar award winning film: Dear Basketball

"It don't means a thing, without the Ring. The playoff starts."

After a nearly 3 month of shut down. The playoff started in the "Orlando bubble" an isolation zone at Walt Disney World in Bay Lake, Florida, with 16 teams competing for one goal, to win the championship

After almost a year of arduous journey. The Los Angeles Lakers became the final champion of this Special and Memorable Season after defeating the Miami Heat in the NBA Finals, winning their 17th franchise title, tying the Boston Celtics as a franchise with the most Championship Titles in the history of the NBA.
